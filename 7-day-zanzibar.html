8-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Pick Up.

Pickup from the airport and transfer to your hotel, Get time to relax after your long trip to Tanzania. Dinner and overnight at your hotel. • Luxury Accommodation : Zanzibar Melia

Day 2: Zanzibar Safari Blue.

Morning after breakfast at 08:00hrs, you will take for Safari blue .Safari blue trip in Zanzibar is one among the best sea adventures in the Zanzibar island, not only sailing along with the traditional dhow but also you can have snorkeling, swim, and relaxing on a white sandy beach on a sandbank. In addition to all, there are plenty of sea foods whom are all waiting for you to be tested, the dhow departs from the fumba or kikungwi beach at 09:00am and we get back around 04:30pm. With your Safari Blue guide, you will explore the mangrove lagoon hidden inside the Kwale Island. Swimming into the protected mangrove forest at high tide is an unforgettable experience. This mangrove lagoon is famous known as natural swimming pool. Later after your excitement drive back to your hotel for dinner and overnight. • Luxury Accommodation : Zanzibar Melia

Day 3: Cheetahs Rock.

A day tour to Cheetah Rock in Zanzibar offers a unique wildlife experience, combining nature, conservation, and stunning coastal surroundings. After a 45-minute to 1-hour drive, you'll explore the nature reserve on a guided safari walk, where you can encounter cheetahs, monkeys, and other wildlife in their natural habitat. If available, you may even have the chance to interact with the cheetahs under supervision. The tour includes a Zanzibar-style lunch and time to explore the reserve’s peaceful environment. It’s a perfect outing for animal lovers and those seeking a more natural side of Zanzibar, with opportunities to learn about conservation efforts and enjoy the island's beauty. • Accommodation: Zanzibar Melia

Day 4: Stone town Tour.

Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage site, is the historic heart of Zanzibar, known for its Swahili architecture, narrow streets, and rich cultural mix of African, Arab, Indian, and European influences. Key landmarks include the House of Wonders, the Old Fort, Freddie Mercury’s House, and the Sultan’s Palace. Visitors can also explore spice plantations, vibrant local markets like Darajani Market, and traditional Swahili houses. Best explored on foot with a local guide, Stone Town offers a chance to learn about Zanzibar's history, shop for handcrafted goods, and experience the island's unique culture. Mornings are the best time to visit, as they are less crowded and cooler. • Accommodation: Zanzibar Melia

Day 5: Full Day at Jozani Forest

Full Day at Jozani Forest A tour of Jozani Forest, Zanzibar's only national park, offers a chance to explore lush tropical landscapes and observe the endangered Red Colobus Monkeys, along with other wildlife like Sykes Monkeys and various bird species. Highlights include a walk along the mangrove boardwalk, ideal for birdwatching and photography. The park, located about 35 minutes from Stone Town, is best visited during the dry season (June to October) and is typically explored with a local guide who shares insights on the ecosystem and conservation efforts. • Accommodation: Zanzibar Melia

Day 6&7: Full Day Relaxing Zanzibar.

Time to spend precious time at one of the best beaches in the world. You will spend these days relaxing at the beach hotel. Any other activities and tours can be arranged by our travel experts; including Spice Plantation, Stone town tour, the Dolphin tour a tour of Prison Island or Jozani forest depending on your preferences. The safari blue or a sunset dhow ride is also other activities that can be arranged. Overnight at a beach hotel, Booked on Half board with dinner and breakfast included. • Luxury Accommodation : Zanzibar Melia

Day 8: Zanzibar/ Departure.

Breakfast at the hotel, then depending on your schedule you will be transferred to the Zanzibar airport for your flight back home.
