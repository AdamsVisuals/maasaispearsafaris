About the Great Migration

The annual Wildebeest Migration is the largest mammal migration on Earth, with over 1.5 million wildebeest, 400,000 zebra and 200,000 gazelle moving in a continuous cycle through the Serengeti and Masai Mara ecosystems.

Our company has specialized in Migration safaris since 2005, with expert guides who know exactly where to position you for the most spectacular wildlife viewing opportunities throughout the year.
